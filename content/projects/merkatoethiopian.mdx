This was a project through Portland's Bureau of Development Services to build a community site for Yadi's Ethiopian restaurant during COVID. The goal was to give the community a gathering place online, somewhere to order food, stay connected, and celebrate the culture. It felt like genuinely useful work at a really hard time.
