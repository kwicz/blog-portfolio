Working on this LMS platform was a truly invigorating experience—one that allowed me to blend innovative technology with a deep commitment to community growth. As a Software Engineer and LMS Consultant, I refined and upgraded a beta LearnDash platform, meticulously customizing features like interactive quizzes, hands-on assignments, and engaging gamification elements to transform the learning journey. I also crafted key WordPress pages using PHP, JavaScript, and HTML, ensuring that every touchpoint—from the inviting landing page to the detailed gamification overview—reflected the platform’s vision for empowering professional development.

The revamped system now seamlessly supports a thriving community of over 20,000 youth champions, educators, coaches, and child care workers. By integrating self-paced courses, live workshops, and credit-bearing programs, the platform offers a uniquely blended learning experience that meets diverse needs and fosters continuous growth. This project not only highlights the power of thoughtful design and technology but also celebrates the collective passion for lifelong learning and community empowerment.
