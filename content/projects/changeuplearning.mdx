This was an LMS consulting project for Change Up Learning, a platform serving educators, child care workers, and youth development folks across the country. I came in to upgrade and customize their LearnDash setup, adding gamification, interactive quizzes, and support for credit-bearing programs alongside self-paced courses and live workshops. Getting all three formats to feel like one cohesive experience was the main challenge, and it was satisfying work.
