Starting Happy Camel Wool was more than just building an e-commerce site—it was about connecting my passion with my purpose. Creating this site to sell unique camel wool socks from Mongolia allowed me to bring something meaningful into the world, while also diving deep into the world of technology. As an ENFJ, I’m continuously inspired by the ability of technology to bring people together. Integrating Stripe for seamless payments felt like a natural step in creating a trustworthy and efficient experience for my customers, reinforcing my commitment to helping others thrive through my work. Through the process of building this site, I realized that software engineering was my calling. It wasn’t just the technical challenges that excited me; it was the opportunity to make a tangible difference through thoughtful and impactful design. Every hurdle I overcame, every line of code I wrote, brought me closer to my goal of aligning my passion for creativity with the practical skills of a software engineer. Above all, this journey reaffirmed how technology can transform an idea into something that resonates—connecting people and driving positive change. This project will always hold a special place in my heart, as it’s where my passion for software engineering truly began.
