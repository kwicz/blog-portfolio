Most chatbot solutions are either expensive, clunky, or both, so I built this one. It's a WordPress plugin that uses Claude to help visitors find relevant articles on your site, built specifically for my company's clients. It was also my first time building anything with generative AI, and it turns out that's a lot of fun to work with.
