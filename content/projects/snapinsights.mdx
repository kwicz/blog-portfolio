I work at a UX agency and researchers there are constantly juggling three different tools to capture and annotate insights. I wanted to fix that. Snap Insights is a Chrome extension that handles screenshots, annotations, and voice notes all in one place. I made the icons and logo myself, and I think they came out pretty cute.
