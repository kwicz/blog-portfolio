This was a team project where I was the "former teacher" on the squad. The idea is that you mess with DNA codon sequences and watch different creatures generate, kind of like Pokémon but actually educational. I brought the learning framework, and the rest of the team covered biology accuracy, creature design, and backend logic. It's not successfully deployed yet, but the idea is genuinely solid and we'd love to revisit it someday.
