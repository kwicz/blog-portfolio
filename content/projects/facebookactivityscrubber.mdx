Facebook Activity Scrubber is a Chrome extension that helps you clean up your Facebook activity log by automating the deletion of posts, comments, likes, and other activities from your profile. It provides a simple, browser-based interface to manage your digital footprint.

The idea came to me after a friend discovered that even though they deleted their Facebook account 15 years ago, it could still be reactivated with all their old activity intact. That experience highlighted the need for a tool that allows people to take control of their digital privacy.

This was my very first Chrome extension, and I had a blast making it. I created the design and icon myself, and it was also my first time registering a domain through Porkbun. I was genuinely impressed with their product and service, which made the launch process smooth and enjoyable.

## Features

- Automates deletion of posts, comments, likes, and activities
- Easy-to-use browser-based interface
- Helps manage and protect your digital footprint
- Lightweight and privacy-focused

## Tech Stack

**Tech Stack:** JavaScript, Chrome Extensions API, HTML, CSS

## Project Story

Building this extension was both a technical and personal project for me. It not only gave me the chance to learn how Chrome extensions work, but it also deepened my perspective on digital privacy. Looking back, this project sparked my interest in creating tools that help people feel more in control of their online presence.
