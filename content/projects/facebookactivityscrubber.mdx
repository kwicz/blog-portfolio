A friend told me that even though she'd deleted her Facebook account 15 years ago, she could still log back in and find all her old posts intact. That seemed bad. So I built a Chrome extension that automates cleaning up your activity log. It was my first Chrome extension and also my first time registering a domain (through Porkbun, who I now recommend to everyone). I made the icons myself too.
