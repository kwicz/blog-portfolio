I built this for my family's dealership because the platform they were paying for looked like it was designed in 2004 (and hadn't been updated since). The whole site is config-driven, so if they ever want to rebrand or open a second lot, I just swap one file. I spent way too long on the SEO slug generator, but watching their Google traffic climb made it worth it.
