I love dogs and I wanted an excuse to play with TensorFlow.js and MobileNet, so this happened. You upload a photo and it tells you the breed. The UI is rough and it's not optimized for mobile, but that was kind of beside the point — I just wanted to see the model in action, and it works surprisingly well.
