I kept running into car dealers who wanted a website but didn't want to sit through a 45-minute sales call to find out what it would look like. So I built a tool where they type in their dealership name and see a real preview instantly. The whole conversion funnel runs through that preview, which felt way more honest than a "request a demo" button. The dark space-y theme was maybe a weird choice for an auto industry product, but I liked it.
